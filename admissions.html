Begin Your Academic Journey at ESSU Can‑avid

At ESSU Can‑avid, we offer a welcoming and supportive environment with academic programs in Criminology, Industrial Technology, Elementary & Secondary Education, and more. Join us—where PASION (Purpose, Accountability, Service, Integrity, Opportunity, and Nurture) leads the way.

How to Apply

1

Fill Out the Admission Form

Complete the ESSU‑ACAD‑100a form (Can‑avid variant is available online/downloadable).

2

Submit Documents

Provide your high school diploma, transcript of records, birth certificate, and certificate of good moral character.

3

Take the Admission Test

The campus conducts its entrance exam between April 21 and June 11, 2025 for Academic Year 2025‑2026; applications are now being accepted.

4

Check Admission Results

Successful applicants will receive their exam result via email or campus bulletin within two weeks after the test.
