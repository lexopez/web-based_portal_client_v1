Program Overview

BSBA Major in Marketing Management

The BSBA Marketing Management major equips students with the knowledge and skills in marketing, advertising, sales, consumer behavior, product management, and strategic planning.

It prepares graduates for careers in marketing, brand management, market research, advertising, and entrepreneurship, enabling them to thrive in highly competitive industries.
