Academic Programs at ESSU Can‑avid

ESSU Can‑avid Campus provides quality higher education programs tailored to local needs in Education, Agriculture, Business Administration, Information Technology, Criminology, and Industrial Technology. Designed to support the community, they prepare graduates for competent service and leadership.

Undergraduate

Business Administration (BSBA)

Major in Financial Management—prepares students for entry-level roles in finance and administration.

  • 4 years
  • B.Sc. in Business Administration
Undergraduate

Elementary / Secondary Education (BEd / BSEd)

Majors offered in Elementary Education, Secondary Education (Math, Social Science, Science).

  • 4 years
  • Bachelor in Education
Undergraduate

Agriculture & Agricultural Technology

BS Agriculture (majors in Agronomy, Animal Science, General Agriculture) and Bachelor of Agricultural Technology.

  • 4 years
  • B.Sc. / BAgri Technology
Undergraduate

Information Technology (BSIT)

Prepares students for careers in software, networking, and IT support roles.

  • 4 years
  • B.Sc. in IT
Undergraduate

Criminology (BSCrim)

Trains students for careers in law enforcement, investigation, and public safety roles.

  • 4 years
  • B.Sc. in Criminology
Graduate

MA in Management

Graduate degree (non-thesis option) offered at Can‑avid Campus Graduate School.

  • ~2 years
  • MA in Management
Graduate

MA in Education

Majors available in Educational Management and Elementary Education.

  • ~2 years
  • MA in Education
